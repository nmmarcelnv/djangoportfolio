About Me

Welcome to my site!

I am a science anthousiast and always fascinated by the idea of conducting science across disciplines. In the past years, I have explored many areas of studies including Physics, Computer Science, Machine Learning, Data Science, Business Analytics, Public Health, and Biology. In this process, I have realized that people in various field face similar problems and that borrowing ideas from one field of study can provide innovative solutions to problems in another field.

I believe Innovation is achieved by thinking out of the box and approaching problems using unconventional methods. I hope browsing through some of my projects will inspire you to think about ways your own skills can be useful in other disciplines.
